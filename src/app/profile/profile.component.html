Sophie's son - Ollie

2014
10 Aug

Visit to the out of hours doctor on a Saturday to get some small symptoms looked at.

13 Aug

Return to the hospital for blood tests and scans. Several hours later we were told he had leukaemia and we were taken straight to Royal Marsden in Sutton.

8 Sep

Day 29 and Ollie is in remission! More work is needed but things are looking better.

2015
25 March

Ollie has started consistently eating and drinking again and has had his NG tube removed. He is looking forward to starting Reception in September without the constant hospital trips.
